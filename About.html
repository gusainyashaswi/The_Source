The Source-The Idea

The idea for The Source emerged from a deep fascination with Uttarakhand—its landscapes, stories, temples, and the unique spiritual energy that defines the region. While brainstorming a game concept, we realized that many games explore fictional worlds, but very few showcase the richness of Indian mountain culture, especially Uttarakhand’s mythology.
We asked ourselves:
What if players could explore Devbhoomi like a real open-world—walking through villages, discovering temples, following legends, and interacting with myths that generations have passed down?
This question became the foundation of The Source.
We took inspiration from:
Local folklore and temple stories,The natural beauty of the Himalayas, Ancient rituals, deities, and river origins, The mystery surrounding the divine energy that flows through the land
What started as a small idea grew into a full concept—an open-world game that blends adventure with culture, mythology with exploration, and reality with imagination.
