Welcome to Devbhoomi

In the heart of the Himalayas, two young adventurers set out across the sacred lands of Uttarakhand in search of The Source — the mythical origin of all creation. Their journey takes them through misty valleys, ancient temples, and forgotten ruins where every step reveals a fragment of truth buried in legend.

But the closer they get, the more they realize that the path to The Source is not just about discovering the world’s beginning — it’s about confronting their own destiny. Every choice, every storm, and every silence will shape the fate of both the seekers and the world they hope to understand.
